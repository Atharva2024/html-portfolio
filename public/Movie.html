My top 3 movies of all time

The Matrix

A mind-blowing sci-fi thriller that explores reality, free will and artificial intelligence, blending action with deep philosophical themes.

Interstellar

A visually stunning sci-fi epic that explores love, time, and survival through the lens of space exploration and relativity.

Inception

A mind-bending sci-fi thriller with deep concepts about dreams and reality, which might intrigue you as a logical thinker.
